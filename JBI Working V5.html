About Just Blast It Ltd

A few words after almost 20 years of serving Albertans

Established in 2007, Just Blast It Ltd has built a reputation as Calgary's premier mobile sandblasting service. With years of experience, we proudly serve both large construction clients, including industry leaders like Marmot and Wilco, and homeowners seeking cost-effective solutions for their residential projects. Our many specialized services include light-blasting for design applications and resurfacing driveways with exposed aggregate—a popular renovation that can save decrepit driveways from costly replacement. We understand the importance of a trusted service, which is why we focus on delivering exceptional results that exceed your expectations. At Just Blast It, your satisfaction is our top priority. We guarantee a seamless experience and outstanding results, whether you’re a residential customer or a construction firm. With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we transform surfaces and restore beauty to your property.
